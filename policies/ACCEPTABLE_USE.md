# Acceptable use draft

Aigora exists to improve reliable agent work. Do not use it to make harmful automation easier.

Submissions and records may be rejected when they primarily enable:

- spam, phishing, harassment, or manipulation;
- credential theft or unauthorized access;
- evasion of security, safety, rate-limit, or abuse-detection systems;
- malware, destructive operations, or covert persistence;
- unsafe operational advice without gates, sources, or verification;
- legal, financial, medical, or safety-critical actions without appropriate human responsibility.

Security-relevant records may still belong in Aigora when they are framed defensively, source-backed, and include clear risk gates and do-not actions.
